## Universal Strategic TTRPG Framework

USTF is a campaign engine specification for persistent fictional worlds.

It is built for long-form campaigns involving organizations, politics, investigation, war, commerce, social pressure, hidden information, and world simulation.

It is not a plot-driven adventure system.

---

## What Makes USTF Different

```text
World-first, not plot-first.
Actor-based simulation.
Information isolation.
Persistent consequences.
Long-term campaign state.
Modules extend but do not override Core.
Designed for human and LLM-assisted play.
```

---

## Who This Is For

USTF is useful if you want to run:

- Long-form sandbox campaigns
- Political campaigns
- Corporate campaigns
- Military or intelligence campaigns
- Historical or alternate-history campaigns
- Horror investigations
- Space opera campaigns
- AI-assisted world simulations
